How to Egg

This is a guide to how to cooked an egg.

Boiled Egg

Hard egg are easy to cook and versatile as it can be use in many dishes of your choice.

Cooking ware and ingredients needed

  1. Pot with lid
  2. Egg(s)
  3. Water


Instructions

Step 1

Put cold water in the pot and lay the egg at the bottom, the water should be 1 or 2 inch above the egg. Then cover the pot with a lid.

Step 2

Bring the temperature to high heat to boil the water and let the egg sit for 12 minutes or so for a hard consistency.

Step 3

Remove the egg from the heat and run cold water on it.

Step 4

Peel the egg and then enjoy.
